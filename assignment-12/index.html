Air Fried Tofu

Ingredients

  • 1 Block of Tofu
  • 4 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 tbsp Hoisin Sauce
  • Pepper

Directions

Cut and drain the tofu and put it in a bowl with all the sauces. Mix them together and then cook it in the airfrier for 15-20min. -Manousos (ps click the text for a suprise)
