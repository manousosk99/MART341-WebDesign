1. What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?
The relationship between an IP address and the DNS is that the DNS connects IP
addresses to domain names. Our computer uses DNS to look up domain names and
get the associated IP address.

2. What is a cookie?
Cookies are files put on computers by websites through the browser. They can
be used in many different ways such as tracking activity.
